FLOAT ON AIR



At GTS we’d like nothing better than to clear the air. Some companies may find your expectations lofty.


But in our view, exceptional, environmentally friendly air cleaning delivered by expert technicians is your due. When you invite us into your building or home, you can expect respectful, reliable and courteous service and a commitment to excellence second to none.


GTS are trained air system cleaners and mould remediation technicians offering in-suite and building-envelope services to property managers and condominium owners across the Greater Toronto Area. We clean appliances, ducts and vents to improve the quality of your home’s indoor air and enhance safety, durability and energy efficiency.


Since cleaning the air by polluting the environment makes no sense at all, we pride ourselves on supporting industry-leading green initiatives. It’s not just that we only use cleansers, filters and other products with recognized green stamps of approval.


But we’re proud of the complete line of services we offer and the significant benefits they deliver. Once we’ve serviced your dryer or HVAC or cleaned the duct and air systems in your home or throughout your building, we’re sure you’ll be floating on air. The atmosphere you breathe will be purer, healthier and safer.
